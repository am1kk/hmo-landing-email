Privacy Policy

For Hear Me Out: Friends Hot Takes

Effective Date: June 12, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Lonely Labs, Inc. ("Company," "we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, discloses, and protects information when you use Hear Me Out: Friends Hot Takes, our website, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

This Privacy Policy should be read together with our Terms and Conditions.

Plain-English Privacy Summary

  • Hear Me Out is an 18+ social voice app. It is not intended for users under 18.
  • We collect account, profile, voice, transcript, social, device, usage, contact, approximate location, support, and payment-related information depending on the features you use and permissions you grant.
  • Voice answers may be transcribed, moderated, ranked, shown to groups or public communities, and made available as anonymized share exports.
  • Anonymized exports are designed not to include your profile name or profile picture by default, but anonymization and voice masking cannot guarantee that you will never be recognized.
  • We do not sell personal information and we do not use your voice recordings to train AI models.
  • You can request account/profile deletion by contacting support@hearmeout.social. We aim to process deletion requests within 30 days, subject to backups, safety, legal, and abuse-prevention needs.
  • You can control permissions such as microphone, contacts, notifications, photos, and location through your device settings.

8.Voice Recordings, Transcripts, Moderation, and Exports

Voice recordings are core to the Service. We may store, play back, transcribe, caption, moderate, rank, and display voice recordings and transcripts to provide the Service. Transcripts may be visible to users, used for moderation, used for search, used for recommendations, and used to improve prompt and feed experiences.

We may use automated systems to help detect prohibited content, abuse, spam, policy violations, safety risks, or technical issues. Authorized personnel may access limited content or account information when necessary for safety, support, legal compliance, abuse prevention, or troubleshooting.

Anonymized exports are designed to remove or avoid the speaker's profile name and profile photo and may use voice masking or other presentation changes. However, no anonymization or masking method is perfect, and someone who knows the speaker or context may still infer identity.

Once content is exported, screen-recorded, copied, or shared outside the Service, we may not be able to delete or control it on third-party services.
